2023年6月Timezone游戏厅畅玩活动


日期
2023年6月17日 3:00 PM — 5:00 PM
位置
Level 3 Forest Hill Chase Shopping Centre
270 Canterbury Road, Forest Hill, VIC 3131

活动内容：

Timezone游戏厅整整两小时游戏无限畅玩！

活动时间安排：

6月17日，3:00-5:00pm

活动地点：

Level 3 Forest Hill Chase Shopping Centre, 270 Canterbury Road, Forest Hill, VIC 3131

交通方式：

Parking

The Forest Hill Chase has over 3200 car spaces with most being under cover and can be accessed off Canterbury Road, we suggest driving around to the back of the parking structure to find the ramp up to level 3 giving you the most direct route to Timezone.

Bus Routes

Bus Routes 703,735,736 and 765 run through Forest Hill Chase.

Train Line

Customers can catch a Ringwood, Lilydale or Belgrave line arriving at either Blackburn or Nunawading Station and catching a connecting bus (listed above) service direct to the centre.

价格：每人$34
